Welcome to Bounce

Bounce is managed by Darren Clunn who is a University trained sport and exercise scientist with international and domestic experience in coaching elite athletes in strength and conditioning. With over 15 years of experience in Physical development, athletic performance, injury rehabilitation and body composition management.

During this time Darren has developed and trained some of world rugby unions best to date this also includes working alongside and in consultation with coaches such as Stephen Larkham, Glen Ella, Eddie Jones and Jake white.

Darren is currently completing a Phd in partnership with the University of ? and also consults to the Ricoh Rugby Union Team in Japan and to local community organisations such as the BDCU committee on youth anxiety and depression and the Bowral Blacks Rugby club.

Having first arrived in Bowral in 1988 Darren has commuted between Canberra and Sydney for both study and work and more recently to Japan where he and his family where based for 3 years. Darren has returned back home to the Southern Highlands where he and his family have established themselves.
